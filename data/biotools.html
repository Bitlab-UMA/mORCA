• Service

    List of available services

    • NetChop

      Neural network predictions for cleavage sites of the human proteasome

    • NetCTL

      Prediction of CTL epitopes in protein sequences

    • RNAmmer

      Prediction of 5s/8s, 16s/18s, and 23s/28s ribosomal RNA in full genome sequences

    • TatP

      Prediction of the presence and location of Twin-arginine signal peptide cleavage sites in bacteria

    • TMHMM

      Prediction of transmembrane helices in proteins

    • FeatureP

      Comparative analysis of protein sequences derived from the same transcript by alternative splicing, genome variation data (SNPs) or protein families from one or more organisms

    • SpindleP

      Neural networks prediction of whether a human gene is located at the metotic spindle

    • NetPhos

      Neural network predictions for serine, threonine and tyrosine phosphorylation sites in eukaryotic proteins

    • NetNES

      Prediction of the leucine-rich nuclear export signals (NES) in eukaryotic proteins

    • NetGlycate

      Prediction of glycation of amino groups of lysines in mammalian proteins

    • LipoP

      Predictions of lipoproteins and discrimination between lipoprotein signal peptides, other signal peptides and n-terminal membrane helices in Gram-negative bacteria

    • ProP

      Neural network prediction of arginine and lysine propeptide cleavage sites in eukaryotic protein sequences

    • ProtFun

      Prediction of cellular role, enzyme class and Gene Ontology category

    • TargetP

      Prediction of the subcellular location of eukaryotic proteins

    • ChemProt

      Ressource of annotated and predicted chemical-protein interactions

    • EasyGene

      Prediction of genes given a sequence of prokaryotic DNA for 138 different organisms

    • GlobPlotter

      GlobPlot performs intrinsic protein disorder, domain and globularity prediction.

    • JasparDB

      Web service interface to the Jaspar database containing profiles of transcription factor binding sites.

    • ELMMatcher

      Mapping of ELM (Eucaryotic Linear Motifs) motifs to a query sequence.

    • ELMInstanceMapper

      The ELM instance mapper uses PHI-BLAST to map ELM predictions of a query sequence to known annotated ELM instances.

    • Jaspar_service

      Jaspar database of transcription factor binding site profiles

    • LiftOver

      This service converts genome coordinates and genome annotation files between assemblies.
